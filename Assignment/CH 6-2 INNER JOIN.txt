2 DISPLAY FIRST_NAME , DEPARTMENT_NAME , DEPARTMENT_ID (USE USING CLAUSE)

4 DISPLAY FIRST_NAME , DEPARTMENT_NAME , DEPARTMENT_ID , BOTH MANAGER_ID (USE USING CLAUSE)

5 DISPLAY FIRST_NAME , DEPARTMENT_NAME , DEPARTMENT_ID , BOTH MANAGER_ID (DON'T USE USING CLAUSE)

6 DISPLAY FIRST_NAME , DEPARTMENT_NAME , CITY (USE USING CLAUSE)

7 DISPLAY FIRST_NAME , DEPARTMENT_NAME , CITY (USE ON CLAUSE)
